My Classmates in Senior High School

A journey that took 12 years, a journey that had so many obstacles, a journey that made me say goodbye to old friends and say hello to the new and unknown ones, a journey that had many tears and frustrations that made me almost give up, in this journey I have been with many classmates all them being unique on their own but this is one batch of my classmate that I enjoyed my time particularly with in this 12 years journey, this batch of classmates of mine are all chaos incarnate I once thought one my classmates as very quite and introverted until I found his real personality who is very loud and loves having fun, they are fun to be with even though sometimes they can be a headache, sometimes they are all noisy and sometimes they are obedient who follow our teachers words, all of them are full surprises that I can’t get enough of, I could even call all of them as my friends. I appreciate my time with this batch because of them they allowed me to crawl out of my shell that I have been protecting myself with from this cruel and unforgiving world and they are also responsible for allowing me to see my potential, I truly appreciate and hold dear to my heart the time that I shared with them.

Successful Research Paper

As the time of my Senior High School is getting closer to the end, our final project which is a research paper. Every single one of my classmates work was overseen by 4 panelist, and the panelist pointed all of the big mistakes in their research paper, and as time goes by and one by one each of my classmates research paper being analyzed, mistakes being pointed out, until it was my groups turn, my heart was pumping hard, my eyes getting dizzy due to the nervousness I felt in the situation so I steeled myself said to my mind that I can do this, as me and my group mates come to the center we presented our research paper to the panelist some of them read our research paper as we ended our presentation all of the panelist read our research paper with great focus, and I don’t know how much time passed until they finished reading the research paper but I can say it is very overwhelming as the thoughts of failure completely fills my mind it was only over until all of the panelist asked us questions on the research paper as time goes by all of the panelists complimented our research paper saying that no are major mistakes that ruins the research and they that our research was a successful, as I stepped outside of the room holding my research called “The effect of weekly reading sessions on Del Sur Good Shepherd Inc Senior High School Students English Reading Comprehension” I screamed joy jumping like a kid who received his favorite in his birthday.
